thought

soda theory

may 26 2025


the fizz : first impressions are crucial in determining your potential.

the taste : once you catch the eye, you gotta match that high expectations.

the length : presentation should be of a perfect length, not longer - not shorter - no useless extras.

the aftertaste : your impression must linger on. achieved generally by connecting to a relatable concept/theme.

the burp : making the ending of the presentation satisfactory, by giving a scenic ending or an interesting cliffhanger/idea.
